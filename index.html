    About Us

    Welcome to Pedro Capri, the Epitome of Gourmet Dining on the Island of Capri

    Perched on the picturesque island of Capri, Pedro Capri is a culinary gem that promises to delight your senses with its exquisite gourmet cuisine and expertly crafted cocktails. As you step into our elegant dining room, you'll be enveloped in a warm and inviting atmosphere that sets the tone for a truly unforgettable dining experience.
    Our menu is a love letter to the rich flavors and traditions of Italian cuisine, with each dish carefully crafted to showcase the freshest ingredients and innovative techniques. From the tender seafood to the rich pasta dishes, every bite is a masterclass in flavor and texture. Our chef's passion for creating unforgettable culinary experiences is evident in every aspect of our menu, from the delicate amuse-bouche to the decadent desserts.
    But it's not just the flavors that will leave you in awe – our presentation is truly exceptional. Each dish is a work of art, with vibrant colors, delicate garnishes, and artistic plating that will make you want to pause and appreciate the beauty on your plate. Whether you're a foodie, a photographer, or simply someone who appreciates the finer things in life, our dishes are sure to impress.

    Savor the Flavors of Italy

    Our menu features a range of delicious options to suit every taste and dietary requirement. From classic Italian favorites to innovative creations, every dish is made with love and care using only the freshest ingredients. Some highlights of our menu include:

    Fresh Seafood: Our seafood dishes are a highlight of our menu, with fresh catches of the day expertly prepared to showcase their natural flavors. Handmade Pasta: Our pasta dishes are made fresh in-house every day, with a range of delicious sauces and toppings to choose from. Mediterranean Inspirations: Our menu features a range of dishes inspired by the flavors and ingredients of the Mediterranean, from grilled meats to fresh vegetables and herbs. Decadent Desserts: Our desserts are a sweet treat, with classic Italian favorites like tiramisu and panna cotta alongside innovative creations.

    Experience the Artistry of Pedro Capri Cocktail Bar

    But Pedro Capri is more than just a restaurant – it's also a sophisticated cocktail bar with a range of expertly crafted drinks to enjoy. Our mixologists use only the finest ingredients and innovative techniques to create unique and delicious cocktails that are sure to impress. From classic favorites to innovative creations, our cocktail menu is the perfect accompaniment to your meal or a great way to enjoy a drink in our elegant bar area.
